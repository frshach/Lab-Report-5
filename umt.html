Universiti Malaysia Terengganu

Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT) is a university in Kuala Nerus, Terengganu, Malaysia. It was formerly known as Kolej Universiti Sains dan Teknologi Malaysia (KUSTEM). UMT focuses on marine science, oceanography, food sciences, and natural resource management.

The university has grown steadily and has become one of the leading institutions in marine-related studies. UMT continues to support research and innovation while contributing significantly to coastal and maritime development in Malaysia.

UMT offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple faculties, aiming to produce graduates who are skilled, knowledgeable, and capable of contributing to the nation’s development.
